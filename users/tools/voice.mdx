---
title: "Voice conversations"
description: "Talk to your AI using voice on supported channels."
---

Voice conversations let you speak naturally instead of typing. WednesdayAI transcribes your speech, processes it, and responds with synthesized audio.

## Supported channels

Voice is available on channels where the adapter supports real-time audio:
- **Discord** — voice channels (requires voice permissions)
- Channels using a custom realtime voice provider installed by your administrator

<Note>
Your administrator must enable and configure the voice feature. If you don't see voice options, contact them.
</Note>

## Starting a voice session

- **Discord:** Join a voice channel and type `/wednesday voice start`
- **Custom channels:** Follow channel-specific instructions from your admin

## During a voice session

- Speak naturally — no push-to-talk by default
- Pause ~1 second to signal end-of-turn
- Say "stop" or "cancel" to interrupt a response mid-stream
- Say "end session" or type `/wednesday voice stop` to finish

## Privacy

Audio is processed in real time and not stored after transcription. Transcripts appear in your session history the same way text messages do.
